The site challenge

Beautiful spaces still need disciplined control.

Many renovation problems do not come from design alone. They come from unclear scope, scattered decisions, weak schedule control, missing site records and poor handover follow-up.

01

Unclear scope

Small missing items become disputes, delays or unplanned cost.
02

Scattered coordination

Clients, suppliers and trades lose track when information is spread everywhere.
03

Weak handover records

Defects, maintenance and final documentation need a clear close-out system.
